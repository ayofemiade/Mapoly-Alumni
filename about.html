About

Celebrating our legacy and building our future together.

Our History

The Moshood Abiola Polytechnic Alumni Association was established in 1979 following the first graduation ceremony of the institution. What began as a small gathering of pioneering graduates has evolved into a robust network of over 10,000 professionals across various industries worldwide.

Our association serves as the bridge connecting past, present, and future graduates of Moshood Abiola Polytechnic. We are committed to fostering a sense of community, promoting professional development, and contributing to the growth and development of our alma mater.

Our Mission

To unite MAPOLY graduates in a vibrant community that fosters professional growth, provides networking opportunities, and contributes to the development of our alma mater and society at large.

Our Vision

To be the most impactful alumni association in Nigeria, creating lasting value for our members and making significant contributions to education and national development.

Core Values

  • Excellence - Upholding the highest standards in all our endeavors
  • Integrity - Acting with honesty and transparency in all our dealings
  • Innovation - Embracing creative solutions and forward-thinking approaches
  • Community - Fostering a sense of belonging and mutual support
  • Service - Giving back to our alma mater and society

Our Journey

1979

Foundation

Establishment of the MAPOLY Alumni Association following the first graduation ceremony.

1985

First National Convention

The association held its first national convention with representatives from across Nigeria.

1992

Alumni Center

Inauguration of the first Alumni Center on campus to serve as a hub for alumni activities.

2000

Scholarship Program

Launch of the Alumni Scholarship Program to support outstanding students.

2008

International Chapters

Establishment of international chapters in the UK, USA, and Canada.

2015

Mentorship Initiative

Introduction of the Alumni Mentorship Program connecting students with industry professionals.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launch of the digital platform and virtual networking events to connect alumni globally.

2023

Present Day

Continuing to grow our network and impact with over 10,000 members worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting the MAPOLY Alumni Association

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly.

Can I schedule an appointment to visit the Alumni office?

Yes, you can schedule an appointment by calling our office or sending an email with your preferred date and time. We'll confirm your appointment based on availability.

How can I update my contact information in the alumni database?

You can update your contact information by logging into your alumni account on our website or by contacting our office directly. We recommend keeping your information up-to-date to receive important announcements and invitations.

How can I get involved with the Alumni Association?

There are many ways to get involved! You can volunteer for events, join a committee, mentor current students, or contribute to our fundraising initiatives. Contact us for more information on specific opportunities.

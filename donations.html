Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donations

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, donations to the MAPOLY Alumni Association are tax-deductible in Nigeria as we are a registered non-profit organization. You will receive a receipt for your donation that can be used for tax purposes.

How is my donation used?

Your donation directly supports the initiatives you select, whether it's scholarships, infrastructure improvements, or specific campaigns. We maintain full transparency with regular reports on how funds are utilized. Administrative costs are kept to a minimum, ensuring that the majority of your donation goes directly to the intended cause.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes, we offer monthly, quarterly, and annual recurring donation options. You can set up a recurring donation by selecting the frequency during the donation process. You can modify or cancel your recurring donation at any time by contacting our office.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processors (Paystack) to ensure your payment information is protected. We do not store your credit card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant systems.

Can I donate if I'm not an alumnus?

Yes! We welcome donations from anyone who wishes to support MAPOLY, including family members of alumni, friends of the institution, corporations, and other organizations. Your support is greatly appreciated regardless of your affiliation.

How can I get a receipt for my donation?

A receipt will be automatically emailed to you after your donation is processed. If you need an additional copy or have not received your receipt, please contact our office at donations@mapolyalumni.org or call +234 801 234 5678.

Have Questions About Donating?

Our team is here to help with any questions about the donation process, specific campaigns, or other ways to support MAPOLY.

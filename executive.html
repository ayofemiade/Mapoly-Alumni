Message from the National President

ALH. AYINDE SOAGA

Class of 1985

As alumni of Moshood Abiola Polytechnic, we carry a proud legacy of excellence, innovation, and service. Our association exists to strengthen the bonds between past, present, and future graduates while supporting our alma mater's continued growth. Together, we can create meaningful opportunities for networking, professional development, and giving back to the institution that shaped our careers and lives.

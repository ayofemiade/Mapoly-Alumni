Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my alumni status?

Your alumni status will be verified through our database using your graduation year and department information. If there are any issues with verification, our team will contact you for additional documentation such as a copy of your certificate or student ID.

When does my membership expire?

Regular and Premium memberships are valid for one year from the date of registration or renewal. Lifetime memberships never expire. You will receive email reminders 30 days before your membership is due for renewal.

Can I upgrade my membership level?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time. If you upgrade from Regular to Premium, you'll only pay the difference between the two membership fees. If you upgrade to Lifetime, a portion of your current membership fee may be credited toward the Lifetime fee.

How do I access the alumni directory?

After your registration is approved, you'll receive login credentials for the alumni portal where you can access the directory. Premium and Lifetime members have enhanced search capabilities and can view more detailed profiles.

Are there chapter memberships available?

Yes, we have regional and international chapters. Your national membership automatically qualifies you to join any local chapter. Some chapters may have additional nominal fees to cover local activities and events.
